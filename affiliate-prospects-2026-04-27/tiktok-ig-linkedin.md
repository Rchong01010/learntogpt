### Patterns observed

- **TikTok creators rarely accept rev-share.** They want flat fees ($500-5K per post). Only the small, hungry ones (50-150K followers) or the brand-aligned ones (e.g. @codewithclaude) are likely to accept lifetime affiliate. Pitch them as "first 50 affiliates get co-branded skill" rather than just rev-share.
- **LinkedIn is the unfair-advantage lane.** Audience = literally the Claude Academy ICP (developers, PMs, AI builders, knowledge workers with corporate cards). $19.99/mo is a rounding error for this audience and 30% lifetime is a unique offer. Aim 60% of outreach budget here.
- **Instagram falls in between.** Reel creators are used to UGC deals, more open to affiliate than TikTok but lower conversion than LinkedIn. The Claude-aligned IG creators almost always cross-post from LinkedIn or YouTube.
- **Charlie Hills is the highest-leverage single target.** Cross-platform, all about Claude, runs an agency, has explicitly written "the 17 Claude skills behind 350K followers." If he says yes, he becomes a case study for the program.
- **Allie K Miller is a moonshot.** 2M cross-platform, but already a paid Anthropic ambassador-ish — likely won't take rev-share but may amplify if pitched as "I built this with Claude Code."
- **Anthropic-employee voices (Boris Cherny, Mike Krieger, Catherine Wu)** are off-limits for affiliate — note them as content reference only.
- **Riley Brown (@rileybrown.ai)** is the clearest TikTok ICP fit — literally has dozens of "Claude Code builds X in 3 prompts" videos. He cofounded vibecode.dev so may have alignment conflict; flag.
- **Agency-managed flag:** none of the candidates below show explicit "for collabs email management@..." gatekeeping. Most have a personal email or DM-direct in bio. Re-verify before pitch.

### Caveats / red flags

- **Riley Brown** runs vibecode.dev — competitive product to Claude Academy if vibecode launches edu. Pitch carefully or skip.
- **@codewithclaude** name is suspiciously close to Claude trademarks — verify it's not an Anthropic-operated account before pitching.
- **TikTok numbers are self-reported via Feedspot** (April 2026 snapshot). Re-verify before sending pitch.
- Anthropic official accounts (@claudeai IG, Claude LinkedIn page) are NOT affiliate candidates — listed for reference only.
